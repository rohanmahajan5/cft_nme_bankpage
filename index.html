Goldman Sachs

Goldman Sachs is a well-known global bank that helps companies and governments with important financial decisions. It offers advice on deals, manages investments, and supports various financial projects. The firm is also active in research and trading, earning a strong reputation worldwide.

David Solomon is the CEO of Goldman Sachs, leading the firm since 2018. He has a strong background in investment banking and has played a key role in shaping the firm's strategy. Outside of finance, Solomon is also known for his interest in music and works as a DJ in his spare time.
